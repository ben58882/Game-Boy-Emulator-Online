How to use

Load ROM picks a .gb file from your device and starts it. Nothing is uploaded anywhere — it runs entirely in your browser.

To continue a battery save, choose Load Save first, then load the matching ROM. Download Save writes the cartridge RAM back out as a .sav you can keep.

Notes

The emulator core is written in Java, compiled to WebAssembly. Sound is not wired up. Provide your own ROMs.
